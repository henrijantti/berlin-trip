How Berlin Is Won

Overall winner: most stars. One star per cleared challenge, seven on offer. Most stars at Sunday brunch takes the title.

Tiebreak: relative overperformance. For each cleared challenge, margin % = (result − target) ÷ target. Reversed for the runs: (target − result) ÷ target. For the Condition: (12 − bodyfat) ÷ 12. Missed challenges score zero. Average across all seven — highest average wins. Beating a 2:00 dead hang by 30 seconds outranks beating a 192 kg deadlift by 5 kg. The mathematics are unforgiving and so is the scorer.

The scorer's rulings on rounding, technique and moral victories are biased and final.
